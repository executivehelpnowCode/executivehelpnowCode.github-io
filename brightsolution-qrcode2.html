How the Launchpad Works

The Launchpad moves your organization through three practical phases: Allow, Guard, and Grow.

1

ALLOW

Vision & Strategy

We begin by aligning your leadership team on the vision. We move your leaders from curious to competent, ensuring they are ready to lead this cultural shift.

  • Discovery & Strategy Session: We review your current corporate goals and technical environment to identify how AI can make the most immediate impact.
  • Sanctioned Tool Strategy: We help you define Microsoft Copilot as your secure Home Base and establish clear rules for how other tools can and cannot be used.
  • Culture Shift: We provide messaging templates your leaders need to foster a safe environment for employee disclosure and adoption.
2

GUARD

Security & Governance

We act as the bridge between your vision and your technical execution. We partner with your IT team or M365 administrators to ensure your data is protected before high-volume usage begins.

  • Technical Implementation Plan: We partner with your IT leads to develop a roadmap for security and governance configurations.
  • Licensing & Readiness Advisory: We help your administrators verify M365 license requirements so you have clarity on costs and tools.
  • Policy & Usage Review: We help update your current AI usage policies to set clear security and professional guardrails.
3

GROW

Adoption & Results

This phase transforms individual curiosity into organizational results, creating a culture where technology consistently serves your mission.

  • Interactive User Training: Live virtual training tailored to your user groups with practical AI skills and high-impact use cases.
  • Standardized License Path: A clear path for employees to earn a paid Copilot license when your organization is ready to invest.
  • Senior Living Use Case Guide: A high-level guide featuring 6 to 8 curated use cases for senior living executives or a department of your choice.
  • Momentum Sustainment: Five curated Microsoft Teams posts or emails with tips, reminders, and practice activities.
